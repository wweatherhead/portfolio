Who do you think you are? I AM!

My name is William Weatherhead, and I'm a senior Undergraduate student at the University of Wisconsin - Madison

Welcome to my portfolio! As I progress through the world of cartography and GIS, I'll post updates on maps I've made or topics I'm exploring! Check out my previous projects!.

Minnesota born and Florida raised, I gained an appreciation for the natural world at a very young age. Any chance I got I would convince my parents to visit the Florida Aquarium to gaze at the fish in wonder. As I grew up, that enthusiasm never faded, and I carry it with me to this day. I absolutely love to travel, and to date I've been to more than 20 countries and most of the United States! All of these experiences led to me caring about where things happen, and with my natural science background and cartographic/GIS skills, I can now discover why things happen.
