The World of Haring

This interactive basemap was my first time constructing a map outside of ArcGIS, and also my first intensive design project. Thanks to MapBox's plethora of options, almost every single aspect of this basemap is constructed in the style of Keith Haring's artworks. Originally inspired by a treasured sweatshirt I received when I was in my teens, this map serves as a challenge of my attention to detail and artistic talent, and really opened my eyes to the lengths that map design can go to.
